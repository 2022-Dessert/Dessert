    • What is De Bon?

    Description of Debon

    De Bon wanted to convey the meaning of selling good desserts, named after de Bon, which means good things in French. It is headquartered in Chungcheongbuk-do University Electronic Information Hall 3 in Cheongju, Korea, and has branches nationwide in Pyeongtaek, Gangneung, and Daejeon, the hometowns of each team member. We are working hard to establish more branches in the country.
    In addition to selling desserts under development by Debon itself, users can customize and order desserts themselves. Users will be able to customise their own desserts to get the design they want, and the recipient of the gift will be able to feel the sincerity. You will feel fun and proud while making dessert.
    Under the vision of growing into a "high-quality dessert cafe," Debon is trying to captivate customers with 100% handmade desserts. Debow growing the harder from now on.
